Under the nerdy looks, there dwells an amusing collection of atoms who takes delight in every perfection and imperfection around. I’m a Physics Grad student at Oxford, who gets high on amazing cloud patterns and colors. I am a highly mood driven system, who also believes in the potential carried within. The little time that the course programme leaves me with is spent mostly with music, books and deep contemplations. I take pleasure gazing at clouds and reading spirituality and philosophy. I’m usually a well behaved function, but with some unpredictable jump discontinuities. And I’m here just to “Think Out Loud” the thoughts that cross my mind; for my own sake and for the people who can relate or appreciate.

"I am not going in circles. I am going upwards. The path is a spiral; and I have already climbed a few steps."
(Adaptation from Siddhartha by Hermann Hesse)
